AI Toolkit Guardrails

Our sentiment experiments are classroom tools meant to help you practice reading the news with a critical lens. They are not trading signals or financial advice.

  • Cross check any AI summary against the original source and at least one trusted market feed before acting.
  • Treat hype-heavy social posts as noise. The demo intentionally downranks clickbait headlines and viral rumor bots.
  • Pause before trading on emotion. Use the worksheet prompts to separate sentiment from fundamentals and your long term plan.
FAQ
Does the AI recommend specific trades?

No. The sentiment endpoint serves static practice scenarios so you can rehearse your analysis process without live-market pressure.

What if a headline sounds too confident?

Assume it is opinionated marketing until proven otherwise. Compare against fundamentals, earnings calls, and risk management guidelines before you react.
