Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

At Investing101, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Summary: We collect minimal personal information (email, name) to provide our educational services. We never sell your data. You can delete your account at any time.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email address - Used for login and communication
  • Display name - Shown on your profile and leaderboards
  • Password - Securely hashed (we never store plaintext passwords)

1.2 OAuth Authentication

If you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive:

  • Email address
  • Name
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Unique OAuth ID - To link your account

We do not receive your Google/Facebook password.

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Portfolio data - Your simulated trades and holdings
  • Performance metrics - For leaderboards and statistics
  • Lesson progress - Which lessons you've completed
  • Calculator usage - Which tools you use
  • IP address - For security and analytics
  • Browser type and device info - To optimize our service

1.4 Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Authentication - Keep you logged in
  • Preferences - Remember your theme and settings
  • Session management - Maintain your connection

2. How We Use Your Information

Purpose Data Used
Account Management Email, password hash, OAuth ID

Leaderboards Display name, performance metrics
Communication Email (for important updates only)

3. Data Storage & Security

3.1 Where We Store Data

  • Database: MongoDB Atlas (encrypted at rest)
  • Hosting: Render.com (HTTPS encrypted in transit)
  • Location: United States data centers

3.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security including:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Password hashing: Using bcrypt with salt
  • Access controls: Limited employee access to data
  • Regular updates: Security patches and monitoring

3.3 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • Personal information is deleted within 30 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be retained
  • Backup copies are purged within 90 days

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Market Data Providers

We use these services to provide real market data:

  • Yahoo Finance - Stock quotes and historical data
  • Polygon.io - Alternative market data
  • Alpha Vantage - Additional financial data

These services may log your IP address when fetching data. Refer to their privacy policies for details.

4.2 Authentication Providers

  • Google OAuth - Google Privacy Policy
  • Facebook OAuth - Facebook Privacy Policy

4.3 News Providers

For financial news articles, we fetch content from:

  • NewsAPI.org
  • TheNewsAPI
  • The Guardian
  • New York Times

We cache articles on our servers. We do not share your viewing history with these providers.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Access & Portability

You can:

  • View your data in your profile settings
  • Export your data (portfolio history, trades) - Contact us to request

5.2 Correction

You can update your:

  • Display name
  • Email address
  • Password
  • Preferences and settings

5.3 Deletion

To delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Click Account tab
  3. Click Delete Account
  4. Confirm deletion

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

5.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of:

  • Email notifications - Unsubscribe link in emails
  • Leaderboard display - Make profile private in settings

5.5 California Residents (CCPA)

If you're a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete your personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we don't sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination

5.6 European Residents (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/EEA, you have rights under GDPR:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to rectification
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

6. Children's Privacy

Investing101 is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will delete it.

7. International Users

Investing101 is operated in the United States. If you access our service from outside the US, your data will be transferred to and processed in the United States.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Significant changes will be communicated via email or prominent notice on the site.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will:

  • Notify you within 72 hours of discovery
  • Describe what information was compromised
  • Explain steps we're taking to address the breach
  • Provide guidance on protecting yourself

10. Do Not Track

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser settings. When DNT is enabled, we:

  • Do not track your browsing across other sites
  • Do not serve behavioral ads (we don't serve ads anyway)

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, concerns, or requests, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@investing101.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@investing101.com
  • Website: https://inv101.onrender.com

For data access, correction, or deletion requests:

  1. Email us with your request
  2. Include your registered email address
  3. We will respond within 30 days
