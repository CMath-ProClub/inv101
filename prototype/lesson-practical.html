Translate strategy into consistent routines.

Practical Playbook gives you the templates, checklists, and automations to keep your plan on track. Everything is built to be reused, shared with teammates, and plugged into your favorite tools.

Module 01

Portfolio maintenance sprint

Structure 30-minute review rituals with a guided agenda. Learn how to triage alerts, rebalance efficiently, and log decisions for future you.

  • Workflow template for monthly and quarterly reviews.
  • Automation recipes to route alerts into your inbox.
  • Case study: from scattered notes to confident cadence.
Module 02

Goal-first planning

Reverse engineer target outcomes into contribution schedules, buffer funds, and milestone celebrations. Track progress visually and get notified when it is time to adjust.

  • Milestone builder with celebratory prompts.
  • Scenario planning worksheet for "what if" pivots.
  • Optional partner flow for collaborative goals.
Module 03

Guardrails & taxes

Put risk rules and tax-efficient habits on autopilot. Learn how to document playbooks and communicate them with advisors or accountability buddies.

  • Risk trigger tracker with escalation tree.
  • Tax checklist synced with the annual calendar.
  • Template library for annual letters to yourself.
Module 04

Rhythm experiments

Stress-test your systems by running two-week experiments that tighten or loosen your routines. Capture insights so the playbook evolves with you.

  • Experiment tracker to log hypothesis, actions, and results.
  • Playbook changelog template for noting wins and regressions.
  • Peer review checklist to gather feedback from your circle.
